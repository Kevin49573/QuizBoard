# 🚀 QuizBoard

### *Roll the Dice on Knowledge*

## 🎯 About The Project

**Gamble & Recall** is an innovative gamified learning platform that turns studying into a cosmic board game adventure. Built for university students across all majors, it replaces boring flashcards with **active recall** and **high‑stakes gambling**—all wrapped in a sleek space theme.

### 🔥 Core Mechanics
- **The Blind Gamble** – Pick a card without knowing if it's Easy, Medium, or Hard. The risk is real, and so is the reward!
- **Dice Roll Movement** – Answer correctly to roll dice and move your pawn step‑by‑step across the board.
- **Special Tiles** – Land on Gold (jump +3), Swamp (slowed next turn), or Mystery (random buff/debuff).
- **Combo Nekat** – Answer 3 correctly in a row to trigger a Super Roll and earn +25 bonus RP.
- **Wager System** – On Hard cards, go *Double or Nothing* to double your rewards.

### 🎮 Game Modes
| Mode | Tiles | Timer | Players |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Solo Sprint** | 15 | 3 min | 1 |
| **Solo Marathon** | 30 | 15 min | 1 |
| **Online Multiplayer** | 50 | 20 min | 2‑4 (Room Code) |

### 🛒 Items & Progression
- **Loaded Dice** (30 RP) – Guarantee a roll of 4 or more.
- **Shield** (25 RP) – Negate one wrong‑answer penalty.
- **Spyglass** (20 RP) – Reveal all card difficulties before you pick.

### 👥 Social & Competitive Features
- **Rival System** – Set a rival and race against their Ghost 👻 on your board.
- **Major League** – Compete against students in your exact major.
- **Hall of Fame** – Achieve a streak of 10 to be immortalized forever.
- **Online Multiplayer** – Create or join rooms with a 6‑digit code.

### 🧠 Learn from Mistakes
After every game, scroll down to the **Review Mistakes** section to see every question you got wrong alongside the correct answers. This turns every loss into a powerful learning moment.
